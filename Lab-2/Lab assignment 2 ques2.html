KR Mangalam University, also known as KRMU, was established in 2013 under the Haryana Private University Act. Located in Gurugram, Haryana, K.R. Mangalam University is a private university. It is a UGC-recognised university and has been approved by several other organisations such as the BCI, FDCI, and ACU.
